  • About Us

  • Why TalentedTenthMeet?

    We saw the disappointment in black professionals not finding someone of their financial status that they can find a deeper connection with.

    If you are an African American who makes over $100,000 a month and wants someone who shares your morals, values, personality and financial wealth then this is the site for you!

  • How successful is TalentedTenthMeet?

    As of November 2018 TalentedTenthMeet has a 90% success rate.

  • How do I accept or decline a match?

    We are not Tinder so no swipping here! Your matches will be displayed in your news feed and if you would like to see new matches simply hit refresh at the top of your feed.

  • Our Matching Proccess

  • How does the matching process work?

    Our site uses the Myers Briggs Personality Test questions to generate your personality type. Upon receiving your personality, we will match you with indiviiduals that meet the three compatible personalities of your type that also have the qualities that you are looking for based on your surveys.

  • How does contacting other users work?

    Unlike like other dating sites, we are not a social media platform. Therefore, we do not allow for messaging on our site. However, we do list contact information for each user per their preferences for you to contact them yourself.

  • How do I link to a file or folder?

    In your profile settings you are able to place any other appropriate information you would like other users to know under the section "Additional Info".

  • Account

  • How do I change my password?

    You can change your password in account settings which is found in the drop down menu of your profile picture to the right. Once inside account settings, select password where you will enter your current password and become prompted to type your desired new password.

  • How do I delete my account?

    We would hate for anyone to delete their account - unless they are happily in a relationship! However, if you would like to delete your account. The "delete account" action/button can be found in the dropdown of your profile picture in profile settings.

  • How do I change my account settings?

    You can change your account settings by selecting your profile picture to display a dropdown menu that says account settings.

  • I forgot my password. How do I reset it?

    Upon reaching our login screen you can select "Forgot Password?" where you can enter your email you registered your account with to recieve a link to reset your password.

  • Payment

  • Can I have an invoice for my subscription?

    If you would like an invoice, you can see access it by clicking on your profile picture and selecting "account settings". In "account settings" select "payment" there you will see your payment history and upcoming charges.

  • Why did my credit card or PayPal payment fail?

    Your payment could have failed due to a server error on our part, inaccurate card information or insufficient funds.

  • Why does my bank statement show multiple charges for one upgrade?

    We are happy that you want more features on our site! If you selected one of larger upgrade packages, it could be that PayPal broke the package up by the features purchaed under the same name.
